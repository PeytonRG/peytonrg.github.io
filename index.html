School of Nursing

October 2019 - Present

Currently in the process of redesigning the website for the Auburn University School of Nursing (AUSON). The goal is to create a more captivating experience than what is currently in place on the current production version of the site. Modern web practices and user interface metaphors such as a large hero units, navigation cards with images, interactive widgets for news, upcoming events, podcasts, etc. My responsibilities include the new homepage and the site header and navigation. I started work on the project in October 2019 with the goal to create a working prototype homepage for AUSON to see what we could offer them when the time came for their full site-wide redesign. Now that my team of co-ops has joined me I am a lead developer on the project.
